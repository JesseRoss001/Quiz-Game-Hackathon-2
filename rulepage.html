Gameplay

  1. Objective: Answer 15 questions correctly in a row to win the 9999 M game prize
  2. Question Format: Four possible answers to a quiz question
  3. Time Limit: 30 seconds to make up your mind
  4. Take the Cash: At any point gamers can opt to cash out with the money that have earned

Earnings

  1. Earning: Correct answers advance contestants up the 'money tree', each level having a specified cash value.
  2. Locks: If a lock point is met and a question is answered correctly. You go home with the locked amount.

Lifelines
  1. 50:50: Two incorrect answers are removed, leaving one correct and one incorrect answer.
  2. Phone-A-Friend: Call a friend for advice for 30 seconds.
  3. Ask the Audience: Audience votes on the correct answer using keypads; results shared with the contestant.
